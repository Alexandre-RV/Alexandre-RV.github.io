Research

Using Large Language Models in Critical Contexts: Robustness, Reliability, and Rule-Following by Construction and by Confinement

Research paper · Thales — Hexaforce

A study of how to deploy large language models safely in critical contexts. It explores making model behavior trustworthy by construction and by confinement, so that robustness, reliability, and rule-following are guaranteed by the surrounding system rather than hoped for from the model alone.
